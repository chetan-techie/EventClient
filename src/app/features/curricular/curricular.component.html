Curriculum

Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan is an English medium school affiliated to the Department of School Education, Government of Karnataka. The Karnataka State Curriculum Framework for the Foundational Stage includes information about the vision for education in the 21st century and the importance of early years for lifelong learning.

In grades 1–4, the core subjects are EVS and mathematics. In grades 5–10, the core subjects are mathematics, science, and social science. Apart from Kannada and English, we also introduce Sanskrit from grade 1. Hindi is introduced from grade 5.

We follow the Karnataka Textbooks from Karnataka Textbook Society (KTS) for all classes. In addition to it, we also use extended resources to give varied learning experience to our students.

The Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (KSEEB) conducts the SSLC board exam once a year for class 10. The Karnataka school Examination and Assessment Board (KSEAB) conducts the SSLC board exam once a year for class 10.
