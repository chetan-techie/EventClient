Admission

Admission to our school is based on merit and we follow a transparent and fair process. Parents can do an online/telephonic enquiry and apply for admission in person. We offer classes from pre-kindergarten to Grade 10. Registration forms are available between 10:00 am and 4:00 pm on all working days at the school office.

Document required for admission are as follows:

  1. Attested photocopy of birth certificate from municipal corporation
  2. Two passport size photographs
  3. Proof of residence
  4. Caste Certificate (BPL/SC/ST/OBC)
  5. Transfer Certificate and Marks Card
  6. Pan Card of parents
  7. Aadhar Card of parents and child
