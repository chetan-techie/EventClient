Kala Bharati

The school of creative and performing arts at Kendra having started over 25years ago offers a wide array of classes encompassing Bharatnatyam, Drawing and Painting, Karnataka Music, Hindustani music, Violin, Tabla, Mandolin, Keyboard, Mridangam, Flute, etc.

In collaboration with Bharatiya Jyothisha Samsthan, Bengaluru, we conduct specialized courses leading to certifications such as “Jyotisha Pravesha,” “Jyothisha Prajna,” and “Jyothisha Acharya” since January 1, 2010. These courses emphasize the traditional knowledge of astrology, offering structured learning pathways for enthusiasts and scholars alike.

Cultural enrichment is a cornerstone of Kendra's activities, highlighted by monthly music programs held in collaboration with Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, Bangalore, and Infosys Foundation. These events, scheduled on the third Saturday of each month, showcase diverse talents in field of art and foster cultural exchange within the community.

WHO CAN ENROLL?

UKG, Class 1 and Class 2 Bharatanatyam, Drawing and Painting, Karnataka Music
Class 3 and above and adults Bharatanatyam, Drawing and Painting, Karnataka Music, Kathak, Hindustani music, Tabala, Violin, Mandolin, Keyboard, Mridangam, Veena, Flute, etc.
  • Parents are also welcome to join the classes. Children get motivated when parents join.
  • Enrollment is also open to anybody who is not studying in Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan school.
  • There is no upper limit of age to join the classes.
  • Two classes in a week will be conducted. Week days and weekend (Saturday & Sunday) options are also available. Your choice of week day or weekend option to be specified during enrollment time.
  • BVB students can join classes soon after the school hours (4:00 PM to 5:00 PM) and then leave for home. We offer weekday batches from 4:30 PM to 5:30 PM for students who are studying in schools other than BVB.
  • Students who are already enrolled in classes outside Kala Bharathi can also join the lessons at Kala Bharathi of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan without any hesitation as they will be enrolled in their respective category and lessons will continue after a short revision.
  • Fee for the year will be collected bi-annually in 2 installments (June and November). There is no admission fee.
ADVANTAGES OF LEARNING FINE ARTS AT BVB KALA BHARATI OVER OTHER CENTERS
  • It is your own school and hence learning and safety is assured.
  • The quality of classes at Kala Bharati of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan is guaranteed.
  • Students will be exposed to stage performances regularly.
  • Children will have a parallel career in future, apart from their academic career.
  • Students' emotional and logical skills improve when they learn art forms.
  • Discipline, Confidence, and communication skills also will improve.
  • Kala Bharathi will be recruiting new teachers along with the existing teachers who are highly professional.
  • Instruments (violin and tabala) can be purchased after completion of the first 5 months of classes as students will be provided instruments in Kala Bharati Classes.
  • Akhila Bharatiya Gandharva Maha Vidyalaya exam and S-Vyasa University exam facility will be provided along with certificate courses by Kala Bharati. These certificates will be helpful for students when they apply to universities in India and abroad.
