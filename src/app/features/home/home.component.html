VISION, Mission and values

Vision — To be a leading educational institution that empowers students to become knowledgeable, ethical and compassionate leaders who make a positive impact on the environment.

Mission — At Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, we strive to provide holistic education that nurtures the intellectual, social, emotional, physical and cultural development of our students.

  • Foster a love for learning and spirit of inquiry through rigorous academic curriculum.
  • Promote the development of character, values and ethical behaviour.
  • Cultivate a sense of social responsibility, cultural awareness, patriotism and global citizenship.
  • Provide a safe and supportive environment.
  • Prepare students for success in a rapidly changing world.

Values — We believe that values, such as respect for elders, gratitude, compassion and integrity, are essential for the holistic development of our students. We strive to inculcate these values through storytelling and role-play. We also provide our students with an understanding of the teachings of great Indian philosophers, and encourage them to reflect on the relevance of these teachings in their lives.

Introduction to the school

Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan School in Vijayanagar Mysuru, is a top player in the category Schools in Mysuru. It was established by Dr. Mathoor Krishnamurthy. Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, Mysuru, was started on June 1, 2003. It runs classes from PRE-KG to Std. X. It is affiliated to Karnataka State Secondary Education Board.
